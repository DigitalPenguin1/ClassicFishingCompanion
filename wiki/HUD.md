# HUD (Stats Overlay)

The Stats HUD is a draggable on-screen overlay that shows your fishing stats in real time while you fish.

---

## Toggling the HUD

| Method | Action |
|---|---|
| Right-click minimap button | Toggles HUD (swaps gear if enabled) |
| `/cfc hud` | Toggles HUD from chat |
| Keybind | Assign one in Key Bindings (see [[Keybindings]]) |

---

## What It Shows

- **Session catches** — fish caught this session
- **Total catches** — all-time catch count
- **Fish/hour** — current session rate
- **Fishing skill** — your current skill level
- **Active lure** — lure name and time remaining (counts down live)
- **Goals** — up to 3 active goals with progress (see [[Goals]])

---

## Goal Colors on the HUD

| Color | Meaning |
|---|---|
| White | In progress (under 75%) |
| Yellow | Getting close (75%+) |
| Green | Complete! |

---

## Moving the HUD

Click and drag the HUD to reposition it anywhere on screen. Its position is saved between sessions.

---

## HUD Styles

Two display styles are available in **Settings**:

- **Bordered** (default) — full backdrop with border
- **Minimal** — borderless, blends into the game world

---

## HUD Height

The HUD dynamically adjusts its height based on how many goals are active — it grows taller as you add goals (up to 3 displayed at once).
